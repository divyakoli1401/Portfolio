Appreciation for Winters

This blog is for the appreciation of those mundane aspects of life that often are overlooked, like the beautiful seasons that we get to experience and live. One of those seasons is winter. For some strange reason I always loved winters. Those long cold winter nights made me feel mystified yet there was something magical about them. The foggy early mornings, cold winter breez running through my face, me getting late for my college wearing my favorite hoodies. Putting my hands in my pockets struggling to find the slightest amount of heat to save them from getting cold..

I've always struggled with perpetual cold hands and feet since I was a child, but still I always loved the cold weather during Winters. I can't quite describe the feeling in words but if I had to assign some word to it, it'll probably be tranquility, the sense of calmness and peacefulness that winter renders being the cold stern season it is, in itself is unreal. Sipping a cup of hot tea sitting in college canteen, in the cold weather is heavenly. I always felt that rainy season was overrated. Rains are so passé for being known as romantic. The real romance lies in the winter nights. And obviously the best part about winters is the Holiday season✨
